Get the best for your business with BM Cargo

Warehouse services

We offer a high quality storage of piece and bulky Cargo, full guarantee of its safety, active manipulation of the goods, made according to the customers’ requirements. Our covered spacious warehouses ensure security of our customers’ cargo regardless of the weather conditions. Locations of our warehouses are near ports making it easier and convenient.

Sea Freight

We prepare shipment documents for our customers and facilitate shipment of goods via the Sea.

Air Freight

We prepare shipment documents for our customers and facilitate the entire shipment process of goods via the Air.

Land Freight

We offer transportation of Cargo once they arrive, from our warehouses in Tanzania to our customer’s doorstep. Our customers do not need to follow us, we follow them wherever they are.

24/7 Support

Receive support from our experts all over the world at every stage of the process, 24/7.
